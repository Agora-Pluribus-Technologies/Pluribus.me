# Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 2, 2026

## 1. Our Privacy Approach

We are committed to data minimization.

We collect only the information necessary to operate the Service and do not engage in advertising, tracking, or profiling.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect the following limited data:

* An opaque user identifier provided by your OAuth authentication provider
* Temporary access tokens required to perform authenticated actions
* Technical information such as IP addresses and logs for security and abuse prevention

## 3. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

* Email addresses
* Real names
* Phone numbers
* Dates of birth
* Marketing or advertising identifiers
* Behavioral tracking data

## 4. User-Published Content

Content you publish through the Service is user-controlled.

If you choose to include personal information in publicly published content, that information becomes public by your own action.

## 5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process personal data based on:

* Contract – to provide the Service you request
* Legitimate interest – for security, fraud prevention, and service integrity

We do not rely on consent for core service functionality.

## 6. Data Retention

Authentication data is retained only while your account is active

Security logs are retained for a limited time and then deleted

Deleted accounts result in deletion of associated service-stored data

## 7. Children’s Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.
If we become aware that such data has been collected, we will delete it.

## 8. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent user data.

Data may be processed by infrastructure providers solely to operate the Service.

## 9. International Data Transfers

The Service may process data in different jurisdictions depending on infrastructure providers.

Appropriate safeguards are used where required.

## 10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

* Access your data
* Request deletion of your data
* Receive a copy of your data
* Object to certain processing

Users may download their data or delete their account through the user settings.

## 11.  Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Changes take effect when posted.
